One of the most well known features of the former ZKO facility is the barcode on the wall at the entrance of ZKO1. There have been a number of posts on the usenet group comp.os.vms like this one and this other one where the translation has been a hot topic.

When ZK originally opened it started out as a 26 character, 7-bit ASCII string that read:

digi tals oftw a
reen gine erin g

Eventually, some time in .... the wall was changed, repainted from orange to grey and the message changed to:
1000011 1110101 1110011 1110100 1101111 1101101 1100101 1110010 1110011 0100000 1010111 1101001 1101110
C u s t o m e r s   W i n
1010111 1101000 1100101 1101110 1010111 1100101 1000100 1100101 1101100 1101001 1110110 1100101 1110010
W h e n W e D e l i v e r
